Welcome

Welcome to Newton Horse Farm, a family-owned horse farm where tranquility meets healing. Our mission is to provide a nurturing environment for individuals dealing with mental health challenges and PTSD. Discover the theraputic bond between humans and horses as we embark on a journey of healing together.


At Newtons Horse Farm Inc., we envision a sanctuary where the timeless connection between families and horses harmoniously unfolds. Our family-owned haven, Newtons Horse Farm, stands as a beacon of hope and healing for those navagating the complexities of mental health and PTSD. Through compassion, understanding, and the profound kinship with our equine companions, we aspire to cultivate a space where transformative journeys towards well-being blossom.


About Us

Established in July 2012, Newton Horse Farm Inc. is a cherished family venture rooted in a profound love for horses and a shared commitment to mental health and well-being. Our journey began with a vision to create more than just a farm but a haven where the therapeutic power of horses converges with compassionate support.


As a family-owned enterprise, we take pride in forstering an atmospere that extends beyond traditional equestrian pursuits. With a keen awareness of the challenges posed by mental health and PTSD, our founders dedicated themselves to building Newtons Horse Farm, a unique space where healing is not only embraced but woven into the very fabric of our equine-centric community.


Over the years, Newtons Horse Farm has evolved into a sanctuary where families, individuals, and our majestic horses coexist in harmony. Our unwavering commitment to providing solace, understanding, and transformative experiences remains at the heart of everything we do. Join us on this remarkable journey, where each hoofbeat echos the resilience and strength of those who seek refuge within our peaceful pastures.
